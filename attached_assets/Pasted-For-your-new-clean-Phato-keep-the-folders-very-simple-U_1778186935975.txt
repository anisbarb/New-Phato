For your new clean Phato, keep the folders very simple.

Use this structure:

phato/
├── public/
├── src/
├── package.json
├── vite.config.ts
├── tsconfig.json
└── README.md

Inside src/:

src/
├── app/
├── components/
├── screens/
├── maps/
├── hooks/
├── services/
├── store/
├── styles/
├── types/
├── utils/
└── main.tsx


---

What each folder should do

app/

Core app structure.

Example:

app/
├── router.tsx
├── providers.tsx
└── layout.tsx

Handles:

routing

global layout

app initialization

theme setup



---

components/

Reusable UI pieces.

Example:

components/
├── SearchBar/
├── Sidebar/
├── BottomSheet/
├── VehicleCard/
├── SeatCounter/
├── SplashDots/
├── ChatTray/
└── RoutePill/

Only reusable UI here.


---

screens/

Actual pages/screens.

Example:

screens/
├── PassengerScreen/
├── DriverScreen/
├── TripScreen/
├── SettingsScreen/
└── ProfileScreen/

Each screen controls layout + logic.


---

maps/

Everything map-related.

Example:

maps/
├── MapView.tsx
├── VehicleMarkers.tsx
├── RoutePolyline.tsx
├── DemandHalo.tsx
├── PickupMarkers.tsx
└── mapStyles.ts

Keep all map logic isolated here.


---

hooks/

Custom React hooks.

Example:

hooks/
├── useLocation.ts
├── useSocket.ts
├── useDriverMode.ts
├── useRoutes.ts
└── useTrip.ts

Used for:

realtime

location

route fetching

driver state

trip logic



---

services/

Backend/API/realtime logic.

Example:

services/
├── socket.ts
├── routing.ts
├── location.ts
├── matching.ts
└── storage.ts

This is the app engine.


---

store/

Global state.

Example:

store/
├── appStore.ts
├── tripStore.ts
├── userStore.ts
└── driverStore.ts

Used for:

user state

trip state

map state

driver mode



---

styles/

Global styling.

Example:

styles/
├── globals.css
├── theme.css
└── animations.css

This keeps the black/white design consistent.


---

types/

TypeScript types.

Example:

types/
├── user.ts
├── trip.ts
├── vehicle.ts
└── route.ts


---

utils/

Small helper functions.

Example:

utils/
├── distance.ts
├── formatTime.ts
├── polyline.ts
└── smoothMotion.ts


---

BEST PART

This structure is:

minimal

scalable

very clean

easy to understand

fast to work with

perfect for Replit

perfect for your Phato idea


Not enterprise mess.


---

Very important

DO NOT create:

100 folders

complex backend folders

microservices

unnecessary configs


Right now your power is:

transport logic

map UX

realtime feel

simplicity


That’s what makes Phato interesting.
